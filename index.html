About Me

Story Behind the Lens

I'm a passionate video editor and cinematographer based in West Bengal, India, dedicated to transforming ordinary footage into visually stunning cinematic content.

With expertise in modern editing techniques, smooth transitions, color grading, and storytelling, I create engaging videos that capture attention and leave a lasting impression. Whether it's wedding films, travel reels, YouTube content, shorts, or commercial projects, my goal is to deliver professional-quality edits that bring your vision to life.
