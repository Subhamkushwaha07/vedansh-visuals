About Me

Story Behind the Lens

I'm a passionate video editor and cinematographer based in Asansol, West Bengal, serving clients across Durgapur, Kolkata, and all of West Bengal, India — dedicated to transforming ordinary footage into visually stunning cinematic content.

With expertise in modern editing techniques, smooth transitions, color grading, and storytelling, I create engaging videos that capture attention and leave a lasting impression. Whether it's wedding films, pre-wedding shoots, travel reels, YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, or commercial projects, my goal is to deliver professional-quality edits that bring your vision to life.
